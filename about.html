About Oxynyc Innovations Private Limited

Oxynyc Innovations is a research and development company specializing in the design and manufacturing of eco-friendly celebration items and consumables. The company leverages advanced biomolecule technology to create biodegradable alternatives to plastic, focusing on sustainability, innovation, and large-scale production.

With a strong market presence and consistently high sales, Oxynyc Innovations is at the forefront of developing sustainable solutions for celebrations and everyday consumables.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the celebration and consumables industry with biodegradable, eco-friendly alternatives, reducing environmental impact.



Our Vision

To become a global leader in sustainable product innovation, offering safe and high-quality alternatives to traditional plastic items.

Technology & Innovation

Oxynyc Innovations is a research-driven company, focusing on advanced biomolecule technology to create sustainable materials for everyday products.

The company's R&D team works on:

  • Developing biodegradable materials for celebration items and consumables.
  • Enhancing durability and usability of eco-friendly alternatives.
  • Exploring new innovations for sustainable product manufacturing.
